‚ùì FAQ

Is it safe?

Yes! Everything runs in your browser, no data is sent anywhere. The Community Library uses Supabase for sharing layouts, but your local edits stay private.

Does it work on mobile?

Best experience on desktop/laptop. Works on tablets. Tight but usable on phones.

Can I edit widget colors/themes?

Not yet - only position and size. Use XCTrack app for detailed settings.

Will my changes work in XCTrack?

Yes! Exports standard .xcfg files that XCTrack can import directly.

What if I break something?

Just press Ctrl+Z to undo, or reload your original .xcfg file. Your original file is never modified unless you explicitly save over it.
